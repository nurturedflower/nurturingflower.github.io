PERSONAL PROJECTS

MY BLOOMING STAR REDUX

This project was a book for a indie author contest held in my state.

It was originally up on my itch.io page, but took it down after a while, but I plan to redo the book at some point.

The book was originally about Hoshi and Erika, two of my OCs from my Nurture OC Project, bumping into each other one day, but getting to know each other, and eventually falling in love with each other over the time they spend together.

Also a Girls' Love work of fiction.

THE WISH REDUX

This project was a visual novel project I was working on for a while, but abandoned it after a bit.

It was originally a visual novel surrounding Wish, my OC from my Nurture OC Project, going through the process of transitioning as a transgender person (FTM), who also happens to get wizard powers from somewhere in his transitioning journey.

The redux will feature elements of LGBTQ+ topics, especially since the characters in the redux are LGBTQ+.

Also a Boy's Love work of fiction.

THE WISH REDUX
(COMING SOON)

NURTURE OC PROJECT REDUX

This project was a project I originally started the day of Porter Robinson's second album, Nurture, was released.

Originally a fan project dedicated to my love and support for the album, I plan to bring these OCs back, but with updated designs, updated backstories, and anupdated plot of the project.

The project redux will also include a lot of concepts for each character, the world in the universe they live in, updated lore, etc.

I've also abandoned this project due to stuff being in the way, but it'll be coming back soon.
